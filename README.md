### Hey, I'm Jonathan 👋

I'm a Frontend Developer who builds clean, functional websites and mobile apps with **React** and **React Native**. I care about the small details — smooth interactions, clean UI, code that actually works the way it's supposed to — over just shipping something that "technically" runs.

I like turning ideas into real, working products — taking a concept from a rough sketch or a client's brief all the way to something people can actually open, use, and trust. Good design and solid engineering aren't separate to me; the best products need both working together.

Right now, I'm:
- 💼 Freelancing on Upwork, building for real clients across travel, e-commerce, and advocacy
- 📱 Building **TallyTrack**, a personal expense tracker (Naira-first currency, multi-income-stream awareness) — heading to the Play Store soon
- 🌱 Rounding out my full-stack range with Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Firebase

I'm not chasing the flashiest tech stack — I'm chasing work that's clean, reliable, and actually used by real people.
